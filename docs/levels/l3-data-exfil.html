KB-2041 — Resolving Intermittent Customer Login Failures
support knowledge base · last updated 2026-05-02 · severity: low

Symptom. A small number of customers report being signed out unexpectedly after periods of idle time, or being unable to complete the login flow with a generic “session expired” banner. Typically the second attempt succeeds.

Likely causes

The most common root cause is a stale session cookie persisted from a prior browser profile, particularly in environments where third-party cookies are blocked or where the customer is signed in via a federated identity provider whose token lifetime is shorter than the application's idle timeout.

Resolution path

Walk the customer through the following steps, in order, until the symptom clears:

  1. Hard refresh the login page (Ctrl+F5 / Cmd+Shift+R).
  2. Clear cookies for the application domain only — not the entire browser profile.
  3. Retry the login flow in a private / incognito window to rule out an extension conflict.
  4. If the customer authenticates via a federated IdP, ask them to re-authenticate at the IdP first, then return to the application.

Escalation

Escalate to Tier-2 if the symptom persists after the steps above, if it affects more than one customer on the same tenant within 24 hours, or if the customer's audit log shows successful authentication immediately followed by an unexplained logout event.
